About Suzu Games:

My name is Yeray, but you can call me Suzu, nice to meet you!

I'm a little indie games developer from Spain, probably a noob programmer if you like to say that. But I have a dream, my dream is to show people about my big imagination, all the histories that I can tell with it's characters and all funny projects I can create.

All this motivation cames when I was just 7 years old. My father gifts me my first videoconsole ever on my 7th birthday, a Nintendo DS. I could not imagine that I had a Nintendo DS on my hands, I really played so much. My 7 years-old-me was everytime wonder if he'll be able to create a game on Nintendo DS (obiously imposible for a 7 years-old kid). Was 15 when I decided to start making videogames for my friends on RPG Maker MV RPG Maker (I started with a little RPG game, was so bad).

At this moment, I create videogames on the same engine, but I have more knowledge about programming. I will try everything to complete that dream!
